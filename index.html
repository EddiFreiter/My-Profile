Hi, welcome to my site!

I am currently working as a Senior Research and Development Lead.
My main focus is on improving processes and systems. I have worked on improving workflows in production or writing software and scripts (using python & MS-SQL) to automate workflows and reduce time spent on manual entry.

Over the 15 years at my current employment, I had the privilege to work on large machinery as well as manage small and large projects. Just before I completed my second diploma, I had the opportunity to start working on a web based software, that assisted our QC department in collecting data. This enabled data collection and reporting (Tools used were C#, ASP.NET, CSLA.NET, MS-SQL). It is a thrill to see a project come to life and save money and resources for the company, but equally I enjoy the hands-on contribution to any project.

For 12 years, on the side of my full-time day job, I was privileged with helping small business with their full Technology needs. I did the classic server, PC, network and phone system support. In addition, my background in electronics helped me to assist the clients with their specialty devices, like CNC machines or hearing testing devices.

Since my parents bought our first family computer, I was hooked on technology. Today, I operate my own "datacenter" located in my basement. It's a training ground to test and "play" with new technologies. I love self-hosting of services that improve my families life. Using my "datacenter" I learned to write scripts in bash to automate processes and learned about hosting, security, Linux Distro's (Debian based), networking and many other topics.
