Hello, welcome to my site!

I am a Senior Research and Development Lead by day and a Full Stack Software Developer by night.
I specialize in improving workflows and machines. I have improved workflows in production and written software and scripts (using python & SQL-Server) to automate workflows, reduce manual entry time or written web app for data entry.

For the past 15 years, I maintained small and large machinery and manage small and large projects. Prior to completing my second diploma, I started working on a web-based software. The goal and purpose of the software was, to assist our QC department in collecting data QC testing data from the field. This software enabled data collection and reporting (Tools used were C#, ASP.NET, CSLA.NET, SQL-Server). I am strongly motivated to see a project come to life through completion and save money and resources for my employer. Equally, I enjoy the hands-on contribution to any project.

For 12 years, outside my day job, I operated my own IT consulting business. I used my expertise to consult clients on purchases of software or hardware products. I am an advocate of open-source software and utilized it as often as possible could to achieve the clients’ goals. I consulting services included Software and Hardware (PC’s, Servers) installation and maintenance, Network configuration, Phone System support, Security Solutions and many more. My clients valued my expanded skillset that I was able to leverage in assisting with interconnection CNC machines or configuring audiology testing equipment.

Since my parents bought our first family computer, I was hooked on technology. Today, I operate my own ‘datacenter’ located in my basement. It's a training ground for testing and ‘playing’ with new technologies. I love self-hosting of services that improve my family’s life. Using my ‘datacenter’ I learned to write scripts to automate processes. I learned about Hosting, Security, Linux Distro's (Debian based), Networking and many other topics. Self-hosting, or the process of setting up services and the endless possibilities of automating my home, are the two hobbies I am passionate about.
